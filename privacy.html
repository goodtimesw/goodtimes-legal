🌸 好時光 GoodTimes — 隱私權政策

Privacy Policy

生效日期：2026 年 3 月 14 日

核心承諾：您的健康資料主要儲存在您的裝置與個人 iCloud 帳號中。若您使用伴侶分享功能，僅您選擇的有限摘要資訊會透過 Apple CloudKit 傳送。我們不會販賣您的任何個人資料。

一、我們蒐集什麼資料

好時光是一款經期追蹤與健康記錄 App。您輸入的資料（包括經期記錄、症狀、情緒、基礎體溫、體重、睡眠、LH / 排卵試紙、哺乳與產後記錄等）主要儲存於：

  • 您的 iPhone / iPad 本機儲存空間
  • 您的個人 iCloud 帳號（若您啟用 iCloud 同步功能）

我們不會出售您的資料，也不會將您的健康資料用於廣告追蹤。

二、HealthKit 資料

好時光可選擇性地與 Apple Health（HealthKit）整合，讀取或寫入以下類型的健康資料：

  • 月經流量紀錄
  • 基礎體溫（BBT）
  • 體重
  • 睡眠分析與 Apple Watch 相關溫度趨勢（若您授權）

所有 HealthKit 資料存取均需您明確授權，且僅用於 App 功能與健康趨勢分析。我們不會將 HealthKit 資料用於廣告或行銷目的。

三、iCloud 同步

若您啟用 iCloud 同步，您的資料會透過 Apple iCloud / CloudKit 在您的個人裝置間同步。iCloud 私有資料庫由 Apple 帳號保護，其他 App 無法讀取；傳輸與儲存由 Apple 加密，但本 App 目前未提供額外的應用層端對端加密。

四、伴侶分享功能

好時光提供「伴侶 / 家人分享」功能，讓您可以選擇性地與另一位使用者分享部分週期資訊。此功能使用 Apple CloudKit：

  • 主動分享：您必須主動產生分享碼並提供給對方
  • 有限資料：僅分享您選擇的週期摘要資訊，不包含詳細症狀、情緒、體溫、性行為等私密紀錄
  • 隨時撤銷：您可以隨時停止分享或重新產生分享碼

五、訂閱與付款

好時光提供 App 內訂閱服務。所有交易透過 Apple 的 App Store 處理。我們不會蒐集您的信用卡或付款資訊，僅透過 StoreKit 驗證訂閱狀態。

六、第三方服務

好時光不使用第三方廣告追蹤 SDK，也不會將您的健康資料提供給廣告商。

七、通知

好時光可發送本地推播通知（經期提醒、排卵提醒、BBT、每日記錄、用藥或智慧洞察提醒等）。這些通知主要在您的裝置上排程。

八、兒童隱私

好時光不針對 13 歲以下兒童設計。我們不會有意蒐集兒童的個人資訊。

九、資料刪除

您可以刪除 App，或使用 iOS 的 App 資料管理方式移除本機資料。若您已啟用 iCloud 同步，建議先在 App 內關閉同步或確認是否需要匯出 JSON 備份，以避免之後重新安裝時再次同步舊資料。

十、政策更新

若本政策有重大變更，我們將透過 App 更新或本頁面通知您。最新版本始終可在本頁面查閱。

🌸 GoodTimes — Privacy Policy

隱私權政策

Effective date: March 14, 2026

Core promise: Your health data is primarily stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account. If you use partner sharing, only the limited summary information you choose to share is transmitted through Apple CloudKit. We do not sell your personal data.

1. What Data We Collect

GoodTimes is a cycle tracking and health logging app. The data you enter, including period records, symptoms, mood, basal body temperature, weight, sleep, LH / ovulation test records, breastfeeding, and postpartum records, is primarily stored in:

  • Your iPhone / iPad local storage
  • Your personal iCloud account, if you enable iCloud sync

We do not sell your data or use your health data for advertising tracking.

2. HealthKit Data

GoodTimes may optionally integrate with Apple Health (HealthKit) to read or write health data such as:

  • Menstrual flow records
  • Basal body temperature (BBT)
  • Weight
  • Sleep analysis and Apple Watch temperature trends, if authorized

HealthKit access requires your explicit permission and is used only for app functionality and health trend analysis. We do not use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing.

3. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data may sync across your personal devices through Apple iCloud / CloudKit. iCloud private databases are protected by your Apple account and cannot be read by other apps. Apple encrypts data in transit and at rest, but GoodTimes currently does not provide additional app-level end-to-end encryption.

4. Partner Sharing

GoodTimes provides an optional partner / family sharing feature that lets you share selected cycle summary information with another user through Apple CloudKit:

  • Opt-in sharing: You must actively generate and share a code.
  • Limited information: Only selected summary information is shared. Detailed symptoms, mood, temperature, sexual activity, and other private logs are not shared.
  • Revocable: You can stop sharing or regenerate your sharing code at any time.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

GoodTimes offers in-app subscriptions. All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect your credit card or payment information and only use StoreKit to verify subscription status.

6. Third-Party Services

GoodTimes does not use third-party advertising tracking SDKs and does not provide your health data to advertisers.

7. Notifications

GoodTimes may send local notifications, such as period reminders, ovulation reminders, BBT reminders, daily log reminders, medication reminders, or insight reminders. These notifications are primarily scheduled on your device.

8. Children’s Privacy

GoodTimes is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete the app or use iOS app data management options to remove local data. If iCloud sync is enabled, we recommend turning off sync in the app or exporting a JSON backup first if you may need to keep a copy, so that old data does not sync back to the app if you reinstall it.

10. Policy Updates

If this policy changes materially, we will notify you through an app update or this page. The latest version is always available here.

11. Contact Us

If you have any privacy-related questions, please contact us:

📧 Email: goodtimesw2026@gmail.com
